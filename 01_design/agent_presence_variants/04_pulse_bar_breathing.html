Pulse Bar (Breathing) LOCKED · Session 10

The canonical agent-presence indicator for EDGE Pulse. A thin Edge-Purple bar lives just below the header on every screen. At rest it is 1px at 15% opacity — calm baseline, Pulse present. When the agent is reasoning, the bar breathes in a slow 2-second sine cycle (15% ↔ 40% opacity, 1px ↔ 1.5px height). When an action is ready, a single 600ms heartbeat fires (peak 80% / 2px), then the bar returns to idle while a persistent numbered badge appears on the Action Queue header button. This is a V1 + V2 hybrid: V1's chrome-top location (present on every surface) + V2's breathing motion vocabulary (organic, attentive — not mechanical, not alarming). Chrome doesn't have a face, so V2's persona drift risk is eliminated.

What this indicator says about Pulse

Amplifies
Alive. The breathing motion is biological — every living thing breathes; the cadence is recognized below conscious thought. Present everywhere. Bar lives on every surface (hero, Action Queue, CEO View, profile views, admin console) — Pulse is never "off" in some views and "on" in others. Persistent on action-ready. The 600ms heartbeat is the moment of arrival; the queue badge holds the result. The user can look up at any time and see "yes, there is something for me" without re-scanning the screen. No persona drift. Chrome doesn't have a face. The breathing pulse is the agent's quality of attention, not a character.
Sacrifices
No per-account locality. The bar can say "Pulse is working" but cannot say "Pulse is working on Helix specifically." V3 (Account-Card Ambient Ring) was the only variant that conveyed this; it is deferred to v1.5+ candidate #11 as a possible secondary indicator alongside the bar. If post-demo use surfaces a felt need for "which account is the agent reasoning about right now?", V3 returns additively, not as a redesign.
Says about Pulse
"Pulse is present. Pulse is breathing. Pulse will let you know when there's something for you." Calm by default. Alive when working. Definitive when ready. No performance of thought, no chat-ification, no chrome-needs-attention. The product's voice embodied as a single thin line.

Composition note

This hybrid combines Variant 1's location (chrome-top bar, persistent across every surface including CEO View) with Variant 2's motion vocabulary (gentle breathing — organic, attentive — rather than the mechanical sweep V1 originally used). Chrome doesn't have a face, so the persona risk inherent to V2's brand-mark-heartbeat is eliminated by relocating the pulse from the brand mark to the chrome bar. The amplitude ceilings (40%/1.5px processing peak; 80%/2px action-ready peak) are deliberate — anything brighter crosses from ambient into insistent, and "alive presence" stops being calm.
